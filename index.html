Do you not see how necessary a world of pains and troubles is to school an intelligence and make it a soul? I have been astonished that men could die martyrs for religion - I have shuddered at it. I shudder no more - I could be martyred for my religion - Love is my religion - I could die for that. I would sooner fail than not be among the greatest. I will give you a definition of a proud man: he is a man who has neither vanity nor wisdom one filled with hatreds cannot be vain, neither can he be wise.
